Who Are We?

At WheelMatch, we provide custom wheel designs taking into account the size, offset and other parameters to ensure the tires and wheels fit perfectly on your vehicle. Our service includes wheel design consultation, designing, fabrication, integration, installation and maintenance of wheel.

Why Choose Us?

security
Our wheel and tyre have a 3 year warranty period. No extra charges needed should re-designing is needed as the wheel and tyre fail to integrate into the vehicle.
build
We have partnerships with big tire companies like Michelin, Bridgestone, Goodyear, Continental Tire and many more. Hence, you may make appointment with them for maintenance if none of our branch is conveniently within your reach.
local_shipping
Need to order spare tires? No worries! We keep records of our customers wheel design. We will make as many spare tires as requested and the best thing, SHIPPING FEE IS ON US!
